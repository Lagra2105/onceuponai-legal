Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Infinity Dev ("we", "us") operates the OnceUponAI mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection and use of personal data.

Information We Collect

When you use OnceUponAI, you provide: child's name, child's age, and story topic. This information is used solely to generate personalized fairy tales and is not stored on our servers.

Third-Party Services

We use OpenAI API to generate story content. Your inputs are sent to OpenAI for processing. Please review OpenAI's Privacy Policy.

We use Apple's StoreKit for subscription management. Please review Apple's Privacy Policy.

Data Storage

Stories are saved locally on your device only. We do not collect or store any personal information on external servers.

Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for use by parents to create stories for children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
